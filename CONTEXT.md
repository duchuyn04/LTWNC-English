# English Learning

This context defines the learning language used across flashcards and study activities.

## Language

**Dictation Content Mode**:
The kind of language unit used for a dictation prompt: either a vocabulary term or its example sentence.
_Avoid_: Dictation Answer Mode, voice mode

## Study Mode Selection

**Study Hub**:
The page at `/Study/{setId}` where a learner chooses how to study a flashcard set. It should surface set context and recommend one primary mode.

**Recommended Mode**:
The study mode selected by default on the Study Hub based on mastery progress and content availability.

**Study Filters**:
Cross-mode filters that narrow which cards are studied: starred-only and unlearned-only. These are not study modes.

**Mode Roadmap**:
A low-priority area on the Study Hub listing study modes that are planned but not yet available.
